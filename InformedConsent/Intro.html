Welcome!

  • Dear participant, thank you for your interest in the experiment! Before we begin, it is important that you are informed about the procedures so please read the following information carefully.
  • Your decisions and information provided will be recorded anonymously.

Purpose of the study and procedure:

  • The objective of this experiment is to better understand decision making in a food shopping context. We are very interested to hear your opinion and experiences, what and why you buy the foods you buy.
  • The task involves a simulated shopping task where you will be presented with 38 binary choices where you will be able to choose between supermarket products that vary in different characteristics.
  • The study will last about {{ C.AvgDur }} minutes.

Voluntary Participation and Withdrawal

  • Your decision to participate in this study is completely voluntary.
  • Participants must be at least 18 years old to take part in the study.
  • At any point during the experiment, you may choose to stop your participation and your data will be discarded.

Confidentiality

  • The only information collected in this experiment will be your answers to the task, which is anonymous.
  • The investigator will safely keep all collected data stored in protected storage.

To participate in this experiment you need to:

  • Be 18 or older.
  • Be fluent in English.
  • Focus only on the experiment (it will take around {{ C.AvgDur }} minutes).
  • Use your computer. The experiment does not work on phones or tablets.
  • Set this window into Fullscreen mode.
  • Agree to start with the experiment right away.

We reserve the right to exclude you from payment if we detect that you are not paying attention. (jumping between other windows or setting off the Fullscreen too many times) or you do not comply with any of the above-mentioned requirements.
